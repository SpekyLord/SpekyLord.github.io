Timeline of Computers: From Childhood to Present

2010 - Introduction to Print Media

As I began to learn to read, children's books and magazines became part of my media consumption. My parents would often buy illustrated storybooks and educational magazines, which introduced me to the world of print media.

2012 - Gaming and Computers

My first real interaction with digital media began when my uncle introduced me to computer games in 2012. This marked the beginning of my fascination with computers and the digital world.

2017 - My First Cellphone

my first cellphone in 2017 was a pivotal moment. It connected me to social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram, and introduced me to the world of mobile apps. This device became my primary medium for consuming news, entertainment, and staying connected with friends.

2021 - Own PC and Advanced Digital Interaction

With my own PC, I delved deeper into the digital world. I started engaging with more sophisticated forms of new media, such as video editing, online gaming communities, and streaming services like Netflix. This period marked my transition from a casual consumer to an active participant in digital media.
